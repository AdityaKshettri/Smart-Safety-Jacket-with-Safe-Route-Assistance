Smart Safety Jacket with Safe Route Assistance App

Let's see how it works!

The objective of our project is to design a multipurpose wearable tech jacket that will assist the user for public safety against health issues as well as crime. Live tracking, temperature regulation, continuous health monitoring, poisonous or odourless gases detection, accident detection etc. are the metrics which will ensure public safety. But instead of keeping any extra kit or baggage, we planned to put our system into a jacket, which will serve both as a wearable as well as a safety gadget.
